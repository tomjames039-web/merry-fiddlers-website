# 🔍 Finding Your £50 Voucher Purchase

## Immediate Action Checklist

### ✅ Step 1: Check PayPal Account
**This is where the money went (or should have gone):**

1. **Log in**: https://www.paypal.com/myaccount/transactions
2. **Check Activity** from the last 7 days
3. **Look for**: £50.00 GBP payment
4. **Payment to**: gourmetburgerco (your PayPal.me link)
5. **Check the payer's details**:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Date/time of payment

**If you find it:**
- ✅ **Payment received!**
- Write down: Customer name, email, date, transaction ID
- You'll need this to send them their voucher

**If you DON'T find it:**
- Customer may have abandoned checkout
- OR payment is still pending
- Check "Pending" transactions too

### ✅ Step 3: Check PayPal Email Notifications

**PayPal sends YOU an email when you receive money:**

**Search your email for:**
- From: "service@paypal.co.uk"
- Subject: "You've got money"
- Subject: "Payment received"
- Amount: "£50.00"

**This email will contain:**
- Customer name
- Customer email (if they included it)
- Transaction details

### ✅ Step 4: What to Do When You Find Them

Once you have the customer's details from PayPal:

1. **Create a voucher code** for them:
   ```
   GIFT50-[TODAY'S DATE]-[RANDOM 4 DIGITS]
   Example: GIFT50-240605-A7B2
   ```

2. **Email them** (use the template below)

3. **Keep a record** in a spreadsheet or document
